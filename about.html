About us

Raybells Restaurant is a family-owned and operated restaurant that has been serving the Nyeri community for over 20 years. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible dining experience, and we take pride in our delicious food, friendly service, and relaxed atmosphere.

We offer a wide variety of dishes, including both Kenyan and international cuisine. We use fresh, high-quality ingredients to prepare all of our dishes, and we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options.

  • Nyeri
  • 4d

Our restaurant is open for lunch and dinner, and we also offer a brunch menu on weekends. We have a spacious indoor dining area, as well as a beautiful outdoor terrace overlooking the Nyeri hills. At Raybells Restaurant, we believe that food is more than just sustenance. It is a way to bring people together and to create lasting memories. We are grateful for the opportunity to serve our customers and to be a part of the Nyeri community.
